Activity Type
The type of activity performed, such as hiking or walking.

Date
The calendar date on which the hike took place.

Favorite
Indicates whether the hike was marked as a favorite by the user.

Title
The custom or system-generated name given to the hike.

Distance
Total distance covered during the hike (usually miles or kilometers).

Calories
Estimated number of calories burned during the hike.

Time
Total recorded duration of the hike.

Avg HR (Average Heart Rate)
The average heart rate throughout the hike, measured in beats per minute (bpm).

Max HR (Maximum Heart Rate)
The highest heart rate recorded during the hike.

Aerobic TE (Aerobic Training Effect)
A score (typically from 0.0 to 5.0) estimating how much the hike improved aerobic fitness.

Avg Cadence
Average number of steps taken per minute during the hike.

Max Cadence
Highest number of steps per minute recorded during the hike.

Avg Pace
Average time taken to cover one unit of distance (for example, minutes per mile or kilometer).

Best Pace
Fastest pace achieved during any portion of the hike.

Ascent
Total elevation gained during the hike.

Total Descent
Total elevation lost during the hike.

Avg Stride Length
Average distance covered per step during the hike.

Training Stress Score (TSS)
A composite score that measures hike intensity and duration to estimate overall training load.

Steps
Total number of steps taken during the hike.

Body Battery Drain
Estimated reduction in the bodyâ€™s energy reserves caused by the hike.

Min Temp
Lowest recorded ambient temperature during the hike.

Decompression
A recovery-related metric estimating how much rest may be needed after the hike, depending on the device ecosystem.

Best Lap Time
Fastest time recorded for a single lap or segment during the hike.

Number of Laps
Total number of laps or segments recorded during the hike.

Max Temp
Highest recorded ambient temperature during the hike.

Moving Time
Total time during which movement was detected, excluding pauses or rest stops.

Elapsed Time
Total clock time from the start to the end of the hike, including all stops.

Min Elevation
Lowest elevation reached during the hike.

Max Elevation
Highest elevation reached during the hike.
